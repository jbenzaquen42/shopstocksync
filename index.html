Built for a simple, reliable inventory workflow

ShopStockSync focuses on the practical tasks a small shop owner needs most: updating listings in batches, catching low stock issues early, and getting a clear snapshot of sales-related activity without exposing private customer information.

📦

Bulk Update Listings

Prepare listing changes from a local spreadsheet and keep product quantities, SKUs, and availability aligned in one pass.

⚠️

Low Stock Alerts

Spot items that are getting close to empty before overselling becomes a problem, with a simple view designed for day-to-day shop management.

📈

Sales Data Overview

See a lightweight overview of listing performance and inventory movement while keeping the workflow centered on your own private records.

Designed for personal use, not a public marketplace app

ShopStockSync is positioned as a lightweight developer utility for internal inventory coordination. It is intended to support a private spreadsheet-based workflow and does not need to collect or expose direct end-user sales data.
